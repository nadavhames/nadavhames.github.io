About

Welcome to my turmite sim!
After taking EECS2001 at York University and learning about models of computation (including turing machines), I decided that it would be apt to try to make something that is both visually interesting and conveys some of the courses concepts. Feel free to try the available presets via the dropdown or make your own instructions.
I hope you enjoy!

Guide

Turmites perform the following operations each timestep: turn on the spot, (by some multiple of 90째) change the color of the current square, and move forward one square in the direction it is facing.
The direction to turn is one of L (90째 left), R (90째 right), N (no turn) and U (180째 U-turn).
This simulator uses the format of |SetColourTo|SetTurnto|GoToState| to tell the turmite where to go: Depending on current colour and state, the corresponding instruction is chosen.
To add or remove states, use the '+' and '-' buttons.
